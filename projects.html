Our Projects

As a newly established company, Golden Blade Development Inc. is currently focused on small-scale, high-impact projects that demonstrate our potential and vision. These include:

  • Rehabilitating damaged urban properties in township and city fringe areas.
  • Land preparation for affordable housing in underutilized rural plots.
  • Pilot partnerships with small-scale farmers and entrepreneurs to boost agricultural productivity and create local jobs.
