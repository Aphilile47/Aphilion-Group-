About Us

Aphilion Inc.™ is the official trademark of Aphilion Group (Pty) Ltd, a youth-led South African corporation dedicated to reshaping Africa’s future through sustainable innovation, smart infrastructure, and inclusive development.

Founded by Mr A. Mzilikazi, Aphilion Group operates across several key sectors — from real estate development and logistics to waste management, technology, and agriculture. Every division is built on a shared mission: to Build Beyond the Horizon and empower the next generation of African innovators.

What began as a visionary idea has grown into a strategic corporation focused on creating value, opportunity, and long-term growth — starting from humble beginnings and expanding toward a sustainable, technology-driven future.
