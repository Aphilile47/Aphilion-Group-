About Us

Aphilion Inc.™, a trademark of Aphilion Group (Pty) Ltd, is a youth-led corporation dedicated to advancing Africa’s growth through innovation, sustainability, and smart development.

Founded by Mr A. Mzilikazi, the corporation focuses on creating generational wealth, empowering local communities, and establishing globally competitive industries across multiple sectors — including real estate, agri-tech, smart vending, and infrastructure innovation.

At Aphilion Inc™, we believe in building beyond the horizon — transforming bold ideas into sustainable realities that uplift lives, strengthen economies, and define the future of Africa’s urban and rural landscapes.
