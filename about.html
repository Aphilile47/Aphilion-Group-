About Us

Golden Blade Development Inc. is a youth-led company committed to reshaping the African landscape through real estate, land development, smart vending machines, and sustainable investments. Founded by Aphilile Mzilikazi, our mission is to build generational wealth, improve communities, and provide inclusive housing solutions across South Africa and beyond.
