About Us

Founded in 2025 by a visionary 18-year-old entrepreneur, Golden Blade Development Inc. is a bold new force in South African real estate. Our mission is to revitalize underused and damaged properties, develop sustainable housing for all income levels, and empower small businesses and farms through strategic investment. We believe in innovation, inclusivity, and impact.
